Frequently asked

Common questions,
direct answers.

Tap any question to expand. If yours isn't here, the waitlist form reaches the founder directly.

What is Shakti and who is it for?+
Shakti is FinLytTech's financial intelligence platform built specifically for India's women entrepreneurs. If you run a tailoring unit, food business, retail outlet, or services firm and use Tally Prime or Zoho Books, Shakti gives you an auto-generated MIS in plain English plus a path to a formal MUDRA loan.
How is Shakti different from the main FinLytTech product?+
Same financial intelligence engine, women-intentional UX. Plain-language labels, vernacular UI, monthly literacy programme, MUDRA-Ready Score, Samuh SHG accounting. ₹299/mo vs. ₹999/mo for the core product — designed for micro-business margins.
What is the MUDRA-Ready Score and how is it calculated?+
A credit-readiness score generated automatically after 6 months of Shakti usage. Six components: business vintage (15%), revenue consistency (25%), cash flow health (20%), debt servicing capacity (20%), debtor quality (10%), SHG track record (10%). Sold as a ₹499 one-time report.
Is the MUDRA-Ready Score a loan guarantee?+
No. It is a credit-readiness indicator and a structured application package. Final loan decisions rest entirely with the bank or NBFC. We are not a lending agent, broker, or loan service provider.
What is Samuh?+
Samuh is Shakti's SHG accounting product for Self-Help Group treasurers. ₹99/month per SHG. WhatsApp-native entry — the treasurer types "Sunita ne ₹500 jamaa kiya" and Samuh posts it. Generates the NABARD compliance report bank linkage officers want. Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi.
Do I need to switch from Tally or Zoho?+
No. Shakti sits on top of your existing books. Your CA continues working in Tally Prime or Zoho Books exactly as before. We read; we don't write.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes — 14 days of Shakti Core (₹299/mo tier), no credit card required. Connect your data and generate your first MIS report before paying anything. Razorpay handles billing.
Is my data safe? Who can see it?+
Your data is encrypted on India-hosted servers (AWS Mumbai), isolated per business, never sold or shared. AI commentary via Anthropic's Claude API — only aggregated figures travel, no transaction-level detail, no customer PII. Full disclosure in our Privacy Policy.
What if I'm a CA serving women clients?+
Our CA Partner Programme white-labels Shakti for your firm — deploy across all your women clients at once, earn 10–15% commission, position your firm as a women's finance specialist. Pricing scales with client count. See the For CAs section above.
What is Maya?+
Maya is Shakti's revenue intelligence layer for Shakti Pro users with 12+ months of data. Analyses customer concentration, seasonal patterns, margin trends, debtor recovery sequences. Outputs growth recommendations grounded in your actual numbers. Bundled in Shakti Pro at no extra cost.
Where can I talk to a human?+
Write to founder@finlyt.net — the founder replies directly within 24 hours (IST business hours). For CA firms exploring the white-label, mention "CA partner" in the subject and you'll be routed to the co-founder.
