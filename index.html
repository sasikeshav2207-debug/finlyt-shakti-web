The problem we solve

Three layers of financial invisibility.
One platform.

India's women entrepreneurs aren't held back by ambition — they're held back by documentation. We've broken the problem into three layers, with a product answer for each. You can step in at whichever layer matches where you are today.

1
Layer One

The SHG treasurer

Manages ₹2–5 lakh of rotating group savings on a paper register. NABARD's e-Shakti collects her data but gives her nothing back. Errors in accounts get her group's bank-linkage loan rejected. Her phone has WhatsApp but no SHG accounting tool.

Our answer → Samuh · digital books in her language, on her phone

The individual entrepreneur

Runs a tailoring unit, food business, retail or services firm. Has a CA filing GST every month. Has never actually seen her own P&L. The CA hands her a computation sheet she can't read. She doesn't know her gross margin, her DSO, or whether she's growing.

Our answer → Shakti · AI MIS in plain English, ratios as traffic lights

Frequently asked

Common questions,
direct answers.

Tap any question to expand. If yours isn't here, the waitlist form below reaches the founder directly.

What is Shakti and who is it for? +
Shakti is FinLytTech's financial intelligence platform built specifically for India's women entrepreneurs. If you run a tailoring unit, food business, retail outlet, or services firm and use Tally Prime or Zoho Books, Shakti gives you an auto-generated MIS in plain English plus a path to a formal MUDRA loan when you're ready.
How is Shakti different from the main FinLytTech product? +
Same financial intelligence engine, women-intentional UX. Plain-language labels on every metric, vernacular UI from day one, a monthly literacy programme, the MUDRA-Ready Score for credit access, and the Samuh SHG accounting module. ₹299/mo vs. ₹999/mo for the core FinLytTech product — designed for micro-business margins.
What is the MUDRA-Ready Score and how is it calculated? +
A credit-readiness score generated automatically after 6 months of Shakti usage. Six components: business vintage (15%), revenue consistency (25%), cash flow health (20%), debt servicing capacity (20%), debtor quality (10%), SHG track record from Samuh (10%). Sold as a ₹499 one-time report that packages the score, scheme match, lender list and projected financials into a single lender-ready application.
Is the MUDRA-Ready Score a loan guarantee? +
No. It is a credit-readiness indicator and a structured application package. Final loan decisions rest entirely with the bank or NBFC you approach. We are not a lending agent, broker, or loan service provider — we just turn your verified financial data into a format lenders accept.
What is Samuh? +
Samuh is Shakti's SHG accounting product for Self-Help Group treasurers. ₹99/month per SHG. WhatsApp-native entry — the treasurer types "Sunita ne ₹500 jamaa kiya" and Samuh posts it to the group's savings ledger. Generates the NABARD compliance report bank linkage officers want. Available in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada and Marathi.
Do I need to switch from Tally or Zoho? +
No. Shakti sits on top of your existing books. Your CA continues working in Tally Prime or Zoho Books exactly as before. We read; we don't write. No double bookkeeping, no migration.
Is there a free trial? How does payment work? +
Yes — 14 days of Shakti Core (₹299/mo tier), no credit card required. Connect your data and generate your first MIS report before paying anything. Razorpay handles billing in INR. Cancel anytime from your account; no questions asked.
Is my data safe? Who can see it? +
Your data is encrypted on India-hosted servers (AWS Mumbai), isolated per business, and never sold or shared. AI commentary is generated via Anthropic's Claude API — only aggregated figures travel, no transaction-level detail, no customer PII, no bank / GST / PAN numbers. Full disclosure in our Privacy Policy.
What if I'm a CA serving women clients? +
Our CA Partner Programme white-labels Shakti for your firm — deploy across all your women clients at once, earn 10–15% commission, position your firm as a women's finance specialist. Pricing scales with your client count. See the For CA Partners section above.
What is Maya? When does it become available? +
Maya is Shakti's revenue intelligence layer — it kicks in for Shakti Pro users with 12+ months of data. It analyses your verified financial history to surface customer concentration, seasonal revenue patterns, margin trends and debtor recovery sequences. Outputs plain-English growth recommendations grounded in your actual numbers, not generic advice. Bundled in Shakti Pro at no extra cost.
Where can I learn more or talk to a human? +
Write to founder@finlyt.net — the founder replies directly within 24 hours (IST business hours). For CA firms exploring the white-label, mention "CA partner" in the subject line and you'll be routed to the co-founder.
