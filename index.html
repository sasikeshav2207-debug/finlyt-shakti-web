Layer 1
A composite

The SHG treasurer

"I keep the savings of fifteen women in my register. The field officer photographs it and leaves. My data goes one way. My group's own accounts never come back to me."

The gap. SHGs aren't taxable entities, so a CA practice can't economically take them on as clients — there's nothing to file. NABARD's e-Shakti collects data extractively but returns no service to the treasurer. The result: no commercial accounting tool was ever built for an SHG treasurer's reality. We built it.

Frequently asked

Common questions,
direct answers.

Tap any question to expand. If yours isn't here, the waitlist form reaches the founder directly.

What is Shakti and who is it for?+
Shakti is FinLytTech's financial intelligence platform built specifically for India's women entrepreneurs. If you run a tailoring unit, food business, retail outlet, or services firm and use Tally Prime or Zoho Books, Shakti gives you an auto-generated management dashboard in plain English and (when you're ready) a credit-ready MUDRA application package — designed for your CA to review and sign off.
How does Shakti work with my Chartered Accountant?+
Your CA continues handling compliance — GST, statutory filings, audit. That's the engagement and it stays unchanged. Shakti sits on top of the Tally / Zoho books your CA maintains and adds the management-decision layer she never had as a separate product. For MUDRA applications, Shakti automates the standardised data assembly so your CA reviews and signs off — instead of building the spreadsheet from scratch.
How is Shakti different from the main FinLytTech product?+
Same financial intelligence engine, women-intentional UX. Plain-language labels, vernacular UI, monthly literacy programme, MUDRA-Ready Score, Samuh SHG accounting. ₹299/mo vs. ₹999/mo for the core product — priced for micro-business margins.
What is the MUDRA-Ready Score and how is it calculated?+
A credit-readiness score generated automatically after 6 months of Shakti usage. Six components: business vintage (15%), revenue consistency (25%), cash flow health (20%), debt servicing capacity (20%), debtor quality (10%), SHG track record (10%). Sold as a ₹499 one-time report that packages the score, scheme match, lender list and projected financials into one lender-ready application.
Is the MUDRA-Ready Score a loan guarantee?+
No. It is a credit-readiness indicator and a structured application package. Final loan decisions rest entirely with the bank or NBFC. We are not a lending agent, broker, or loan service provider.
What is Samuh?+
Samuh is Shakti's SHG accounting product for Self-Help Group treasurers. ₹99/month per SHG. WhatsApp-native entry — the treasurer types "Sunita ne ₹500 jamaa kiya" and Samuh posts it to the group's ledger. Generates the NABARD compliance report bank linkage officers want. Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi.
Do I need to switch from Tally or Zoho?+
No. Shakti sits on top of your existing books. Your CA continues working in Tally Prime or Zoho Books exactly as before. We read; we don't write.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes — 14 days of Shakti Core (₹299/mo tier), no credit card required. Connect your data and generate your first dashboard before paying anything. Razorpay handles billing.
Is my data safe? Who can see it?+
Your data is encrypted on India-hosted servers (AWS Mumbai), isolated per business, never sold or shared. AI commentary via Anthropic's Claude API — only aggregated figures travel, no transaction-level detail, no customer PII. Full disclosure in our Privacy Policy.
I'm a CA. How does the partner programme work?+
Our CA Partner Programme white-labels Shakti for your firm — deploy across your woman-led clients at once, earn 10–15% recurring commission, position your practice in the fastest-growing borrower segment. Pricing scales with client count. See the For CAs section above or write to founder@finlyt.net with "CA partner" in the subject.
What is Maya?+
Maya is Shakti's revenue intelligence layer for Shakti Pro users with 12+ months of data. Analyses customer concentration, seasonal patterns, margin trends, debtor recovery sequences. Outputs growth recommendations grounded in actual numbers. Bundled in Shakti Pro at no extra cost.
Where can I talk to a human?+
Write to founder@finlyt.net — the founder replies directly within 24 hours (IST business hours). For CA firms exploring the white-label, mention "CA partner" in the subject and you'll be routed to the co-founder.
