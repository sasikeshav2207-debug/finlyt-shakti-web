The problem we solve

Three layers of financial invisibility.
One platform.

India has 12 million SHGs (88% women), 20% women-owned MSMEs on Udyam, and MUDRA disbursements of ₹23.2 lakh crore. Yet women receive only 7.09% of formal MSME credit. The gap isn't ambition — it's documentation. Shakti closes all three.

Layer 1

The SHG treasurer

Manages ₹2–5 lakh of rotating group savings on a paper register. NABARD's e-Shakti collects her data but gives her nothing back. Errors in accounts get her group's bank-linkage loan rejected. Samuh gives her digital books in her language, on her phone.

Layer 2

The individual entrepreneur

Runs a tailoring unit, food business, retail or services firm. Has a CA filing GST. Has never seen her own P&L. Shakti gives her an AI MIS in plain English, her ratios as traffic lights, a one-click investor-ready PDF.

Layer 3

The credit applicant

Eligible for MUDRA, Stand-Up India, PM Vishwakarma. Has 3 years of GST returns and a Tally ledger. Cannot convert any of it into a lender-ready application. MUDRA-Ready Report auto-generates the full package for ₹499.
