Privacy Policy

FinLytTech Shakti · A vertical of Finlyt Technologies LLP · DPDP Act 2023 aligned · Effective: June 2026
Plain English: Your financial data — books, ledgers, customer details — stays yours. We use it only to generate your MIS and (if you opt in) your MUDRA-Ready Report. We never sell it, never use it to train AI models, and never share it with lenders unless you explicitly request that as part of a loan application.

1Who we are

Data Fiduciary: Finlyt Technologies LLP (the LLP behind FinLytTech and Shakti). Founder & CEO: Sasidharan V (CA, IIMA MBA). Co-founder: CA Komal Jagdish Vishwakarma. Registered correspondence: founder@finlyt.net.

2What we collect

From you on signup: name, email, mobile number, business name, optional PAN / GSTIN / Udyam number. For Samuh: SHG name, member names, savings + loan records (entered by the treasurer).

From your accounting system: if you connect Tally Prime, we read XML exports you provide. If you connect Zoho Books, we use OAuth read-only access to pull invoices, bills, ledgers, and reports.

Auto-collected: standard web analytics (page views, browser type — anonymised), and operational logs needed to deliver the service.

3What we don't collect

  • We do NOT collect Aadhaar numbers.
  • We do NOT collect bank account login credentials or net-banking passwords.
  • We do NOT scrape customer-of-customer personally identifiable data beyond party names already in your accounting books.

5How we use your data

  • Generate your MIS reports, ratios, ageing, AI commentary.
  • Compute your MUDRA-Ready Score (only on your explicit request).
  • For Samuh: produce your group's accounts and NABARD compliance report.
  • Send transactional emails (welcome, billing receipts, password reset).
  • Operational analytics — improving the product. Always aggregated. Never individually identified.

6What we never do

  • Never sell your data to anyone.
  • Never use your data to train AI models (Anthropic's API terms also prohibit this).
  • Never share with lenders unless you explicitly download and forward a MUDRA-Ready Report yourself.
  • Never use your data for marketing to third parties.

7Your DPDP rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all your data, in 30 days.
  • Correct — fix any inaccurate data we hold.
  • Erase — delete your account and your data. Cancellation triggers a 30-day export window, then full deletion.
  • Withdraw consent — pause AI processing or full account, anytime, from your account settings.
  • Nominate — appoint a person to act on your behalf in the event of incapacity.
  • Grievance — write to founder@finlyt.net. We respond in 7 working days.

8Samuh — SHG-specific data handling

For SHG treasurers using Samuh: member savings and internal loan entries are visible only to the treasurer's account and to authorised group members the treasurer adds. NABARD field officers see only the auto-generated compliance report you choose to share — Samuh never auto-publishes your data to NABARD or any third party.

9Children

Shakti is not directed at users under 18. We do not knowingly process data of minors.
